Our Portfolio Philosophy

Brands That Earn Their Place

At THJP Enterprises, our brands represent a commitment to quality, reliability, and long-term partnerships. We carefully select and develop relationships with products that meet the evolving needs of the Australian woodworking market.

Each brand within our portfolio reflects our focus on innovation, trust, and service excellence — ensuring consistent performance and genuine value for the retailers and craftspeople we serve.

We do not take on brands for volume alone. Every label in our portfolio must meet our standard of quality, have a clear market fit, and be supported by a supply chain we can stand behind unconditionally.

For brand enquiries: If you represent a quality woodworking brand seeking Australian distribution, we'd love to discuss a partnership.

Vinnie's Oil® Australia

Vinnie's Oil Australia is THJP Enterprises' flagship distribution brand — representing our commitment to delivering high-quality, toxin-free wood oils, waxes, and finish solutions across the Australian market. Developed in Sweden in 2021 by four master woodworkers, Vinnie's Oil is the strongest natural hardwax oil system available anywhere.

Focused on performance, reliability, and 100% food-safe formulation, the brand supports a wide range of woodworking applications by providing products engineered to meet demanding professional standards. Through our exclusive relationship with Vinnie's Oil Europe, we ensure Australian retailers have consistent, reliable access to the full range.

  • Ultra Penetrating Oil (UPO) — flagship penetrating hardwax oil, 270ml
  • UPO Rustic Dark — warm dark-tone finish for aged and reclaimed timber
  • Eternal Seal — invisible topcoat barrier for maximum surface protection
  • Universal Monocoat — all-in-one single-coat system for interior timber
  • Hard Wax & Soft Wax — most concentrated wax finishes on the market
  • Product bundles and branded accessories

Represent a Brand We Should Carry?

We are actively seeking relationships with quality woodworking brands that are looking for authorised Australian distribution. If your product line meets our quality standards and has a clear fit with the Australian market, we'd love to open a conversation.

We look for brands that share our values: product integrity, consistent supply, and a genuine commitment to the craftspeople who use their products.

What We Look For

  • Premium product quality with demonstrable industry recognition
  • Consistent manufacturing and supply chain reliability

  • Commitment to long-term partnership, not transactional supply
  • Pricing structure that works for the Australian wholesale model
