Company Overview

About THJP Enterprises

Importing excellence into the Australian woodworking trade — a specialist B2B distributor built for retailers, not the public.

What We Do

A Business Built for the Trade

THJP Enterprises Pty Ltd is an Australian-owned importer and distributor of high-quality woodworking equipment, tools, accessories, finishes, and specialty timber products. We operate exclusively as a business-to-business (B2B) supplier — supporting a nationwide network of branded retailers across Australia and New Zealand.

We do not sell directly to end users. Instead, we partner with wholesale retail businesses to ensure products reach woodworkers, craftspeople, and hobbyists through established local channels staffed by knowledgeable professionals.

Not a bulk timber supplier. Our focus is specialty timber pieces, craft and project materials, and value-added timber products — distinct from structural timber wholesalers.
Our Approach

Built on the Right Foundations

Everything we do is guided by a belief: the woodworking trade deserves specialist partners, not generalists.

Retailer First

Our business model centres entirely on empowering our retail partners — they know their customers, and we know how to supply them exceptionally well.

Specialist Knowledge

Deep focus on woodworking means every product decision is informed by genuine trade expertise — not a broad general catalogue approach.

Global Reach

Through established manufacturer relationships across Europe, Asia and beyond, we bring the world's best woodworking products to the Australian market.

Australian Owned

Proudly Australian-owned and operated, with a genuine understanding of the local trade, standards, and retail environment.

Product Integrity

We stand behind everything we distribute. Consistent quality and reliable performance are our baseline requirement — not optional extras.

Long-Term Thinking

We build lasting relationships with our retail partners, investing in mutual success rather than transactional supply arrangements.

The Process

How We Operate

1

Source & Import

We identify, evaluate, and import woodworking products from trusted global manufacturers — managing all logistics and compliance to bring product into Australia efficiently.

2

Quality Assurance

Every product is vetted for consistency, performance, and suitability for the Australian and New Zealand market before reaching a retailer's shelf.

3

Wholesale Distribution

We supply exclusively to our trusted network of wholesale retail partners — never directly to end consumers or hobbyists.

4

Retailer Support

Our retail partners receive the product knowledge and supply reliability they need to serve their customers with confidence and expertise.
