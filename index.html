Who We Are

More Than a Distributor

THJP Enterprises Pty Ltd is an Australian-owned importer and distributor dedicated entirely to the woodworking trade. We bridge premium international manufacturers with the retail professionals who serve Australia's craftspeople.

From precision hand tools and machinery to specialty timber products, wood oils, and finishing solutions — our portfolio covers every corner of the woodworking supply chain.
