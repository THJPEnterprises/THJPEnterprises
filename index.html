Company Overview

THJP Enterprises
Pty Ltd

What We Do

THJP Enterprises Pty Ltd is an Australian-owned importer and distributor of high-quality woodworking equipment, tools, accessories, finishes, and specialty timber products. We operate exclusively as an import business-to-business (B2B) supplier — supporting a nationwide network of branded retailers across Australia and New Zealand.

We do not sell directly to end users. Instead, we partner with wholesale retail businesses to ensure products reach woodworkers, craftspeople, and hobbyists through established local channels staffed by knowledgeable professionals.

Our Product Range

THJP Enterprises connects international and domestic manufacturers with the Australian retail market by supplying woodworking machinery and equipment, hand tools and precision instruments, woodturning accessories and components, finishing products including oils, waxes and coatings, and specialty timber products such as pen blanks and craft materials.

The company focuses on reliable supply, consistent quality, and strong retail partnerships across every segment of the trade.

Not a Bulk Timber Supplier

THJP Enterprises Pty Ltd is not a bulk or structural timber supplier. While we distribute selected timber-based products, our focus is on specialty timber pieces, craft and project materials, and value-added timber products for specific woodworking applications. This ensures a clear distinction from traditional timber wholesalers.

Who We Work With

Our wholesale network includes independent woodworking retailers and wholesalers, and domestic and international small, medium, and larger suppliers who share our commitment to quality and service excellence in the woodworking sector.

Distribution Model

How We Operate

A clean, focused model designed to serve retailers and ultimately benefit the craftspeople who depend on them.

1 Source & Import

We identify, evaluate, and import woodworking products from trusted global manufacturers — managing all logistics and compliance to bring product efficiently into Australia.

2 Quality Assurance

Every product in our portfolio is vetted for consistency, performance, and suitability for the Australian and New Zealand market before it reaches a retailer's shelf.

3 Wholesale Distribution

We supply exclusively to our network of trusted wholesale retail partners across Australia — never directly to end consumers or hobbyists.

4 Retailer Support

Our retail partners receive the product knowledge and supply reliability they need to serve their customers with confidence and expertise.
